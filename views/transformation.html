WHAT DO THESE LEVELS MEAN??
When reporting your experience level, please consider the following definitions to maintain consistency in our data:
RANK YOUR SKILLS:

Level 0 | NO EXPERIENCE \\ No experience in this area Level 1 | COMPETENT "\\ Have done some work in this area \\ Am familiar w/basic principles and ideas; but have not delivered work \\ Would require additional support to deliver work "
Level 2 | PROFICIENT You can do most things with the tool or capability as a developer or functional SME. You may not be able to be the Senior resource or lead an effort in this space, but you can be a solid contributor to the team and add significant value.
Level 3 | ADVANCED You can serve as the Senior resource on a team using this tool, or leveraging this capability. You can own scope, solve all of the problems that come up, resolve issues independently, and drive impact.
Level 4 | MASTERY "You have absolute mastery over this tool or capability. You can teach it. You live and breath it. It's in your bones."

RANK YOUR INTEREST:

Level 0 | AVOID Do not possess skill Possess this skill but prefer to do less or no work in this area Would not like to be staffed on projects requiring this skill
Level 1 | DEVELOP Do not currently possess this skill but would to develop new skill Would be interested in Ed/Ex or internal learning opportunities to develop this skill
Level 2 | ENGAGE Possess this skill and would like to keep doing work in this area to leverage skills Happy to be staffed on projects using this skill
Level 3 | ACCELERATE Possess this skill and would like to grow skills further in this area Happy to be staffed on projects using this skill Would like to help develop IP, POV’s, and teach other’s this skill
