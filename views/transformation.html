SEO/SEM \\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, SEO/SEM campaigns. This should include organic and paid search strategies.
\\ Experience managing SEO/SEM execution and performance using solution providers that help optimize for search (e.g. - Brightedge, MOZ, etc)
\\ Experience developing content and targeting strategies for optimizing search engine performance including landing pages, copywriting, cross-linking, etc.




Social \\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, social media editorial calendars and publishing plans.
\\ Deep knowledge of how to leverage the different social media platforms effectively (e.g. - maximizing engagement on Facebook vs Twitter, etc.)
\\ Knowledge and experience in how to effectively leverage a paid social media strategy to drive demand and conversion.




Display \\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, campaign plans for Display advertising. This should include media mix strategy, budget planning, sourcing, etc.
\\ Experience planning and optimizing programmatic/RTB media campaigns
\\ Knowledge of the ecosystem and required components to successfully deliver on Display media campaigns (e.g. - DMP, DSP, SSP, etc)




Email marketing \\ Knowledge and direct experience in how to properly plan, build and execute email marketing campaigns (including landing pages, HTML emails, analytics, personalization, optimization, etc)
\\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, email marketing campaigns within leading tools (e.g. - Salesforce, Marketo, Pardot, Hubspot, etc)
\\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, analytics and reporting to show effectiveness of email campaigns




Multi-channel marketing \\ Hands on experience directly managing, or overseeing teams responsible for building out, cross channel marketing strategies and campaign plans.
\\ Experience and depth of knowledge with the use of different media channels -- what are strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, etc (e.g. - What are the trends for using a multi-channel strategy that balances traditional TV with social media, when to use one over the other, what are the trade-offs, appropriate message types, etc)
\\ Experience sourcing creative and content to run across multiple channels (e.g. - What agency work is needed, what outputs and what formats, what are lead times for delivery, etc)
